Hi, thanks for checking out my site. I'm a human living in Minneapolis who enjoys taking photos with film. Part of my artistic practice includes dropping off the roll at my local Walgreens. I enjoy witnessing the complete bewilderment of the stoned teenager who assures me that everything will be fine or the blank stare of the stoic photo clerk who looks as if they were born in the Walgreens and have lived many years behind the counter. Sometimes the film is scanned upside-down and backwards. In general they do ok, but the mystery is part of the process.

This site was created as a student project. Just FYI people of the world.
