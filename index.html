Welcome to my photography site! Photography has been a hobby of mine since I was about 13 years old when I started taking photos for the junior high yearbook. These days I like to carry a simple point-and-shoot film camera around wherever I go. I enjoy taking photos of my everyday life, my home, my friends and my travels. For the purposes of the this project I have only included a small selection of my photography, if you are interested please check out my blog. Enjoy!

Check out the rest of the site for some of my favorite up-and-coming photographers (Matt Bower, Susan Elizabeth & Anna V Shelton) and some of the famous photographers I look to for inspiration (Nan Goldin, Michael Wolf & Joel Sternfeld).
