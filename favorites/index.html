My Favorite Photographers

There are so many great photographers out there, but there are a few amazing ones that are always inspiring me with their new work. I picked just three of them to feature on this site. I wanted to focus on contemporary, working photographers, who have styles that I try to emulate with my own work. I'm particularly drawn to scenes of nature and quite moments of everyday life captured on film. (Photo above by Matt Bower)

Matt Bower

I discovered Matt Bower's work on Flickr several years ago and it stuck with me. It hard for me to explain what is so captivation about his photos, but they make me nostalgic for a life I've never lived.

Susan Elizabeth

Susan is actually an friend from college and a fantastic photographer. Her dreamy photos make me long for life in San Francisco.

Anna V Shelton

Anna is another photographer I discovered through Flickr. Her photos of the Pacific Northwest are some of my all time favorites.
